<p>We are incredibly proud to announce that two of our own — <strong>Elke</strong> and <strong>Amielle</strong> — have been selected to the <strong>Sydney Comets U14 Girls Representative Team</strong> for the 2026 season.</p>

<p>This is a significant achievement, and one that deserves to be recognised and celebrated. Selection to a representative team at this level is not given — it is earned through months and years of committed, disciplined development.</p>

<p>Congratulations to both athletes, and to their families who have supported them along the way.</p>

<h2>What Makes a Rep Player?</h2>

<p>Elke and Amielle didn't become representative players overnight. Their selection is the result of qualities that we try to develop in every player who walks through the doors of a ProBall Academy session:</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Work ethic</strong> — The willingness to show up, work hard, and keep going when things get difficult</li>
  <li><strong>Coachability</strong> — The ability to receive feedback openly and apply it — not just once, but consistently over time</li>
  <li><strong>Consistency</strong> — Turning up week after week, session after session, and building a track record of genuine engagement with their development</li>
  <li><strong>Resilience</strong> — Bouncing back from setbacks, tough sessions, and disappointing results without losing their drive to improve</li>
  <li><strong>Growth mindset</strong> — Believing that ability is developed rather than fixed — and backing that belief with action</li>
</ul>

<p>These values are not specific to basketball. They are life skills. And we are proud that both Elke and Amielle have demonstrated them in abundance.</p>

<h2>How ProBall Develops Players for Representative Basketball</h2>

<p>At ProBall Academy, our programs are designed to develop the complete player — not just the skills that look impressive in isolation, but the total package that selectors at representative trials are looking for.</p>

<p>Our approach focuses on:</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Skill development</strong> — Comprehensive technical work covering shooting, ball-handling, footwork, passing, and finishing</li>
  <li><strong>Footwork and movement</strong> — The athletic foundations that allow technical skills to work under pressure, against live defenders, in competitive game situations</li>
  <li><strong>Confidence and decision-making</strong> — Training environments that challenge players to make real decisions under pressure, building the composure that shows up in trials and games</li>
  <li><strong>Basketball IQ</strong> — Understanding of spacing, reads, positioning, and how to function effectively within a team structure</li>
  <li><strong>Supportive training culture</strong> — A community of athletes who push each other, support each other, and collectively raise the standard for everyone in the program</li>
</ul>

<p>This environment is what prepares athletes to be <strong>Built Different</strong> — to arrive at a representative trial not hoping they get picked, but knowing they are ready.</p>

<h2>Introducing the ProBall Rep Membership</h2>

<p>Off the back of this announcement, we are also excited to share details of a new membership option we have created specifically for players who have already been selected to representative teams.</p>

<p>The <strong>ProBall Rep Membership</strong> is now available. Here is what you need to know:</p>

<h3>What It Is</h3>
<p>This is not a trial program. It is not a pathway to representative selection. The Rep Membership is designed exclusively for athletes who have <strong>already been selected</strong> to a representative team and are looking for a flexible, high-quality training environment to complement their rep schedule.</p>

<h3>What It Includes</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Full access to ProBall Academy sessions across all our Sydney locations</li>
  <li>Flexible scheduling that works around representative training and game commitments</li>
  <li>A reduced membership rate that reflects the training load that representative players are already carrying through their rep program</li>
</ul>

<h3>Who It's For</h3>
<p>This membership is for confirmed representative players who want to continue developing with ProBall alongside their rep commitments — without paying a full membership rate that doesn't reflect their actual availability.</p>

<p>If your child has been selected to a representative team for the 2026 season and you'd like to find out more about the Rep Membership, reach out to us directly. We'd love to continue being part of their development journey.</p>
