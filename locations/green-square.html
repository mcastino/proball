Basketball Classes for Kids in Green Square

About the Programme

ProBall Green Square is based at Green Square Public School in Zetland, making it the closest ProBall venue for families in the rapidly growing Green Square, Zetland, Waterloo and Alexandria neighbourhoods.

We run both Academy skills training and Miniball games at this venue. Sunday morning Miniball is a popular choice for families looking for a structured activity that doesn't take up the whole weekend. Our Academy sessions run throughout the week for players wanting to train more often.

ProBall Green Square is one of the original ProBall Miniball venues and currently holds #1 Google rankings for "miniball Green Square" — built on years of consistent, quality coaching in the community.
