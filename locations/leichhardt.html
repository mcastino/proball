Basketball Classes for Kids in Leichhardt

About the Programme

ProBall Leichhardt runs at Sydney Secondary College in the Inner West — one of our most established venues. We offer both Academy skills training and Miniball games, making it the most complete ProBall experience available in one location.

Families from Leichhardt, Annandale, Lilyfield and across the Inner West choose this venue for its convenient Saturday Miniball sessions and weekday Academy classes. Whether your child is picking up a ball for the first time or working towards representative basketball, there's a programme for them here.

ProBall Leichhardt has been running longer than any other ProBall venue and is the original home of our Miniball programme in Sydney.

Programmes at This Venue

Miniball

Ages 5–10 · Weekends

Weekly 40-minute modified games during school terms. No scorekeeping, no pressure — just fun basketball with friends. First 4 weeks are free for new members.

Academy

Ages 5+ · 7 days a week

Structured skills sessions with nationally certified coaches. Six specialised class types covering shooting, ball handling, 1v1, game strategy and more.
