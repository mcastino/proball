Basketball Classes for Kids in South Sydney

About the Program

ProBall South Sydney runs Academy skills sessions at South Sydney High School, offering structured junior basketball for players aged 5 and up in the southern suburbs.

Families from South Sydney, Matraville, Mascot and surrounding areas choose this venue for its quality coaching and central location within the south Sydney corridor. Our coaches work with players of all ability levels — from beginners to those working towards representative basketball.

ProBall's flexible membership carries no lock-in contracts and gives access to all 7 Sydney venues.
